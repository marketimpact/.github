# Code of Conduct

MarketImpact work should be professional, respectful, and focused on the humanitarian and development communities we serve.

Expected behavior:

- Communicate with care and specificity.
- Assume good intent while addressing concrete problems directly.
- Protect confidential, beneficiary, client, and operational information.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or public disclosure of private information.

Report concerns privately to `thomas@marketimpact.org`.
